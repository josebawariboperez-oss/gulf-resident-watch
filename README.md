# Gulf Resident Watch

Real-time Iran war impact dashboard for residents of the Gulf states (UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia).

## What this is

An independent conflict impact tracker focused on what matters to people living in the Gulf:
- **Air Defense Data** — Verified interception figures from official Ministry of Defense communiqués
- **Civil Status** — Airport operations, port status, energy infrastructure, civil defense alerts
- **Energy & Oil** — Brent crude tracking, Strait of Hormuz status, supply chain disruptions
- **Statements** — Key declarations from all parties with source attribution

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from official government communiqués and verified news agencies:
- UAE Ministry of Defence (WAM)
- Qatar Ministry of Defence (QNA)
- Bahrain Defence Force General Command
- Kuwait Ministry of Defence
- Saudi Ministry of Defence
- US CENTCOM
- UKMTO, Lloyd's List, Reuters, Al Jazeera, CNBC, Bloomberg

## Tech Stack

- React 18 + Vite
- Deployed on Vercel
- No external dependencies beyond React
